Discipline, Unity, and Service

B.N. College maintains active Army and Navy wings of the National Cadet Corps. Cadets receive rigorous physical training, participate in state and national camps (Republic Day Parade, Thal Sainik Camp), and obtain 'B' and 'C' certificates, paving pathways into the Indian Armed Forces.

Certs & Training

Training schedules cover drill operations, weapon handling, map reading, field craft, and leadership seminars.

Key achievements

Cadets regularly represent Bihar & Jharkhand directorate at the Republic Day Parade in New Delhi.
