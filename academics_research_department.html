Academic Inquiries & Research

B.N. College fosters a vibrant research culture among faculty and scholars. With multiple UGC-supported developmental grants, the college has established research labs in Chemistry, Botany, and Physics, producing high-impact publications annually.

Key Focus Areas

Advanced materials synthesis, environmental botany, electronics design, and modern Hindi & English literary critiques.

Academic Journals

The college publishes two primary research periodicals, Anunaad (Science) and Anveshika (General Research).
