Legal & Regulatory Framework

As a constituent unit of Patna University, Bihar National College Patna is governed by university statutes, state legislation, and national higher education guidelines.

  • Patna University Act, 1976

    Primary legislation detailing the administrative and academic functioning of the university and its constituent colleges.

  • UGC Act Section 2(f) & 12(B)

    UGC recognition ensuring funding eligibility, development grants, and affiliation compliance.

  • Bihar State University Statutes

    Guidelines relating to service conditions, examinations, student welfare, and administrative operations.
