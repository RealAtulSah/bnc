About Anunaad

Anunaad is an annual science and technology journal published by the Science Faculty of Bihar National College. The journal provides a medium for faculty members, research scholars, and students to publish their research, reviews, and insights on modern science topics, including Physics, Chemistry, Zoology, Botany, and Information Technology.
