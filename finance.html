Financial Administration & Funding

The financial operations of B.N. College are managed in strict compliance with the audit guidelines of Patna University, the Department of Higher Education (Govt. of Bihar), and the University Grants Commission (UGC).

Funding Sources

  • UGC Development & Research Grants (including CPE status funds)
  • State Government deficit funding support and staff salary allocations
  • Self-financing student course fees (BCA, BBA, Biotechnology vocational sections)

Finance Committee

Composed of the Principal, senior professors from Patna University, and finance officials, the committee reviews budgets, procurement needs, and tender awards.
