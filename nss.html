Social Service & Extension Activities

The NSS wing of B.N. College fosters community service and civic consciousness among students. Through our two active units, volunteers participate in literacy drives, environmental conservation, health camps, and blood donation campaigns in Patna.

Weekly Activities

Cleanliness drives under Swachh Bharat Abhiyan, tree plantations on campus, and local village adoptions.

Special Camps

7-day annual outreach camps providing health screenings, hygienic sanitation education, and educational kits to rural children.
