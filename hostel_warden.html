Student Accommodations

B.N. College offers residential boarding facilities for male students in the college hostel. The hostels are located close to the campus, offering clean rooms, common recreation halls, dining facilities, and 24/7 security.

Admission Rules

  • Hostel allotments are strictly based on academic merit and distance criteria.
  • Residents must follow all discipline codes; ragging or late hours entry is strictly prohibited.
  • Applications must be submitted to the Warden's Office during academic registration.
