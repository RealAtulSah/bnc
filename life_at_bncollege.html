Vibrant Campus Community

Life at B.N. College extends far beyond classrooms. Situated on the bank of the holy Ganges River, our student community participates in diverse sports, debates, community outreach, and annual fests, maintaining a rich legacy of academic camaraderie.

Social Work

Outreach campaigns driven by active NSS units, teaching underprivileged children in local sectors.

Creative Expression

Poetry, fine arts, and theatre productions featured in the annual college magazine and cultural fests.

Sports Legacy

Tournaments in cricket, football, and badminton, utilising extensive playground facilities.
