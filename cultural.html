Nurturing Talents & Creative Expression

B.N. College hosts vibrant cultural events annually. Through collaboration with cultural organisations like SPIC MACAY, the college provides opportunities for students to perform classical music, dance, theatre, and visual arts, fostering a rich appreciate of heritage.

Annual College Fest

Inter-department fests featuring debates, fine arts exhibitions, street plays, and classical singing competitions.

National Days Celebration

Patriotic poetry reciting, cultural dramas, and lectures hosted on Independence Day and Republic Day.
