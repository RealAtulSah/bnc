Examination Rules & Conduct

The Office of the Controller of Examinations at B.N. College oversees the execution of Patna University mid-semester internal tests and end-semester examinations.

Exam Conduct Rules

  • Students must bring a valid University Admit Card and College ID Card to enter the examination halls.
  • Possession of mobile phones, smartwatches, or any electronic communication equipment is strictly prohibited and treated as malpractice.
  • A minimum of 75% classroom attendance is mandatory to download the exam admit cards.
