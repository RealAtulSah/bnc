Financial Support Systems

B.N. College helps eligible students obtain state and central government scholarships to pursue their higher education.

  • Bihar Post Matric Scholarship (PMS)

    Available for SC, ST, EBC, and BC category students of Bihar, offering fee reimbursement support.

  • National Scholarship Portal (NSP)

    Central sector scholarship schemes for college students based on intermediate merit percentiles.

  • Patna University Merit Scholarships

    Academic excellence awards granted to top rankers in university semester exams.
