Self-Appraisal & Performance Reports

To maintain high educational standards and satisfy UGC CAS (Career Advancement Scheme) requirements, all faculty members of B.N. College must submit their Performance Based Appraisal System (PBAS) self-appraisal reports annually.

Key Parameters assessed

  • Teaching hours completed and innovative curriculum delivery methods.
  • Research publications (peer-reviewed, UGC Care journals) and books authored.
  • Participation in university exams evaluation and administrative committee duties.
  • Contributions to co-curricular and extension services (NCC/NSS counselling).
