UG Registration & Eligibility

Admissions to standard B.A. (Hons) and B.Sc. (Hons) courses are processed through Patna University's unified registration system.

Eligibility Criteria

  • B.Sc. Honors: Minimum 45% aggregate in Intermediate Science (12th class) with physics, chemistry, maths, or biology.
  • B.A. Honors: Minimum 45% aggregate in Intermediate (Arts/Science/Commerce) from a recognized state or central board.

Documents Checklist

  • 10th & 12th Marks Sheet and Passing Certificates
  • College/School Leaving Certificate (CLC/SLC)
  • Migration Certificate (for non-BSEB boards)
  • Category certificate (SC/ST/EBC/OBC, if applicable)

Apply Online

Admissions for the 2026-30 academic session are processed online via the PU Samarth Portal.
