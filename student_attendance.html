Mandatory 75% Attendance Rule

According to Patna University regulations, students are required to maintain a minimum of 75% attendance in both lectures and practical/laboratory sessions in each paper.

Consequences of Short Attendance

  • Students with less than 75% attendance will not be allowed to submit university examination forms.
  • Attendance is logged via biometric fingerprint scanners situated across departments.
  • Relaxation of up to 15% may be granted strictly on medical grounds, supported by valid certificates.
