Zero Tolerance Policy

B.N. College maintains a strict zero-tolerance policy against any form of ragging on campus or in college hostels. Any student found guilty of ragging, abetting ragging, or participating in active harassment will face immediate suspension, rustication from Patna University, and penal proceedings under IPC regulations.
