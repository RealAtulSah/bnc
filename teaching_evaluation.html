Academic Assessment Framework

B.N. College follows the Choice Based Credit System (CBCS) prescribed by Patna University. Evaluation is divided into Continuous Internal Assessment (CIA) and End-Semester University Examinations.

Internal Assessment (30%)

Composed of internal mid-semester exams (15 marks), seminars/quizzes/assignments (10 marks), and classroom attendance (5 marks).

University Exams (70%)

End-semester written theory examinations and practical/viva assessments conducted by Patna University.
