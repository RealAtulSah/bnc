Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC)

The IQAC core team at B.N. College oversees the execution of academic and administrative quality benchmarks. The cell compiles institutional quality metrics, manages student feedback analysis, and designs the Self Study Reports (SSR) for NAAC assessment cycles.

NAAC Grading Details

The college was accredited with a B++ Grade in its first assessment cycle by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council. Current efforts and self-study reports are active for the next accreditation cycle.
