Campus Discipline & Code of Conduct

Students enrolled at B.N. College Patna must adhere to the academic and behavioral codes prescribed by the college administration and Patna University.

Code of Conduct

  • Students must wear their official College Identity Cards at all times on the campus.
  • A minimum of 75% classroom attendance is mandatory to register for semester exams.
  • Any participation in political vandalism, ragging, or drug abuse on campus is treated as a punishable offense.
