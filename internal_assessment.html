Continuous Internal Assessment (CIA)

B.N. College uses continuous evaluation. Out of the 100 marks for each theory paper, 30 marks are allocated to the Continuous Internal Assessment (CIA).

Distribution of 30 Marks

  • Mid-Semester Written Test: 15 Marks
  • Seminars / Assignments / Quizzes: 10 Marks
  • Punctuality & Classroom Conduct: 5 Marks
