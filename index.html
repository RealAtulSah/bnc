Welcome to B.N. College

Bihar National College (B.N. College), Patna is one of the oldest and most prestigious constituent units of Patna University. Established in 1889, the college has a long-standing tradition of fostering intellectual vigor and nationalist spirit. Located on the beautiful banks of the River Ganges near Gandhi Maidan, the college offers undergraduate degree programs in Humanities, Science, Social Sciences, and advanced vocational streams such as BCA, BBA, and Biotechnology.
