Career Development Opportunities

The Training and Placement (T&P) Cell at B.N. College acts as a bridge between academics and corporate industries. The cell organises regular workshops on interview preparation, resume writing, group discussions, and technical screenings, specifically for BCA, BBA, and Biotechnology vocational students.
