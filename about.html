Overview

Bihar National College (B.N. College) is a premier higher education institution of Patna, Bihar. Founded in 1889 by the noble brothers Babu Bisheshwar Narayan Singh and Babu Shaligram Singh of Kulharia Raj, Bhojpur, Bihar, the college was established during the colonial era to offer high-quality education and stoke nationalist feelings among local students.

The college currently operates as a constituent unit of Patna University. It has been recognized as a "College with Potential for Excellence" (CPE) by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and has been accredited with a B++ grade.

Prestige

B.N. College has been a learning center for a galaxy of intellectual gems, including eminent judges, writers, and national politicians.

Diverse Curricula

From traditional sciences and humanities to modern vocational programs (BCA, BBA, Biotech), we serve a wide range of academic pursuits.

Holistic Growth

Active NSS, NCC, and cultural cells ensure students grow socially, physically, and morally alongside their academic achievements.
