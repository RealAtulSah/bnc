Sports Excellence & Facilities

B.N. College has a proud athletic legacy. Our students regularly represent Patna University in national cricket, football, and athletic meets. The campus features sports fields, indoor game setups, and dedicated coaching guidance.

Moinul Haque Legacy

Named in memory of our illustrious principal, Dr. Moinul Haque, who led the Indian Olympic Team at international games.

Key achievements

Patna University Football Champions, golf team representations, and multiple medals in national athletics tournaments.
