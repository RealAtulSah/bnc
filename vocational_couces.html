Vocational Degree Guidelines

B.N. College offers professional self-financing undergraduate honors degrees in Computer Applications (BCA), Business Administration (BBA), and Biotechnology.

Entrance Examination

Admissions are finalized based on the Patna University Common Entrance Test (PUCET) ranking, followed by a viva-voce/personal interview conducted by the respective department coordinators.

Eligibility Requirements

  • BCA Hons: 10+2 with Mathematics/Statistics as a compulsory paper, securing at least 45% aggregate.
  • BBA Hons: 10+2 in Science, Arts, or Commerce streams with a minimum of 45% aggregate.
  • Biotechnology Hons: 10+2 in Science with Biology, securing at least 45% aggregate.

Self-Financed Portal

Vocational registrations are integrated into the Patna University portal. Check schedules for PUCET exam entries.
