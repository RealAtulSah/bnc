Tribute to Hindi Sahitya

By Language Faculty Scholars

B.N. College has been a pioneer in nurturing modern Hindi, Urdu, and English literary creations. Renowned figures like Dr. Rameshwar Singh Kashyap, the legendary creator of "Loha Singh", have graced this college as faculty members. This column features reflections on their creations and hosts new poetry entries submitted by students.

Poetry Corner

"Growing with knowledge, blooming with peace,
Learning never ceases, boundaries decrease."

— Rahul Kumar, BA English (Hons)
