Bachelor of Science (B.Sc. Hons)

A 4-year undergraduate degree program following the Choice Based Credit System (CBCS) framework of Patna University.

Honors Disciplines: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Botany, Zoology, Geology, Statistics.

Bachelor of Arts (B.A. Hons)

Comprehensive liberal arts education covering humanities, languages, and social inquiries.

Honors Disciplines: History, Geography, Economics, Political Science, Psychology, Sociology, Philosophy, English, Hindi, Sanskrit, Urdu, Maithili.

Vocational Honours Degrees

Professional, career-oriented vocational programs supported by dedicated labs and corporate linkages.

Programmes: BCA (Computer Applications), BBA (Business Administration), Biotechnology, Functional English.
