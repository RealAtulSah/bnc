Mandatory Attendance Guidelines

As per the directives of the Department of Higher Education, Govt. of Bihar, and Patna University, B.N. College implements biometric fingerprint attendance logging for all faculty members, support staff, and students.

Rules and Compliance

  • All staff members must log their attendance twice daily: at arrival (before 10:15 AM) and departure (after 4:00 PM).
  • For students, a minimum of 75% biometric attendance registration is mandatory for semester exams and scholarship eligibility.
  • Biometric terminal centers are available in the Main Admin block, Science block, and BCA Vocational department.
