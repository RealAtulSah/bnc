Welcome Back Alumni

The B.N. College Alumni Association provides a platform for former students to reconnect with their alma mater and contribute to its development. Our alumni network stretches across the globe, comprising prominent political leaders, distinguished judges, research scholars, and industry professionals.

Key Goals

  • Fostering networking opportunities between current students and alumni.
  • Supporting college infrastructural and academic development through donations.
  • Organising annual alumni meets to celebrate heritage and achievements.
